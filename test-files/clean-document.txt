Employee Handbook - Company Policies
=====================================

Section 3: Workplace Guidelines

Work Hours:
- Standard business hours: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM
- Lunch break: 12:00 PM - 1:00 PM
- Flexible start times available with manager approval

Dress Code:
- Business casual attire Monday through Thursday
- Casual Friday dress code
- Professional attire required for client meetings
- Company logo shirts provided for team events

Communication:
- Check email at least twice daily
- Respond to urgent messages within 4 hours
- Use appropriate channels for different communication types
- Maintain professional tone in all business correspondence

Benefits:
- Health insurance coverage starts after 90 days
- 401(k) matching up to 6% of salary
- Paid time off: 15 days first year, 20 days after year 2
- Professional development budget: $2,000 annually

Office Resources:
- Kitchen facilities available on each floor
- Quiet zones for focused work
- Conference rooms bookable through calendar system
- IT support available 8 AM - 6 PM weekdays

Performance Reviews:
- Annual reviews conducted in December
- Mid-year check-ins scheduled in June
- Goal setting sessions at beginning of each quarter
- Career development discussions with HR

Contact Information:
HR Department: hr@company.com
IT Support: itsupport@company.com
Facilities: facilities@company.com

This handbook is updated annually and employees will be notified of any policy changes.
